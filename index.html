About Me

My name is Darakhshan Ahmed, and I am a proud mom of 2 beautiful girls who are turning 10 and 3 this October. My youngest is currently starting school and my oldest is starting to become an independent young lady. Now that my children are getting older and becoming self-sufficient, I am ready to take on new challenges and work to learn, explore, and better myself! For the past few years, I’ve been working part-time and taking online courses while my children have been growing up, and as I’ve started to see my kids needing me less and less, I’ve realized that I can begin to take on new challenges and explore the open horizon!


In the past, I started a project for an online platform that enabled tutors and students to meet one another on the web, with the goal of maximizing opportunities between both by 1) giving students a selection of tutors which could be filtered to best fit their needs and 2) connecting tutors to students who needed exactly what they offered. Due to time and budget constraints and a lack of expertise, however, I couldn’t reasonably continue to pursue this project while supporting myself and my family. I’m currently looking for guidance and/or other contributions to redirect the trajectory of this venture, so please don’t be afraid to contact me or look into this project further.
